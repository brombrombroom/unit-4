King of Numbers

Your goals is to match the given number

There are four random picture below. By clicking on a random picture you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching the total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each random picture is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each random picture.
